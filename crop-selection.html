Understanding Environmental Factors

Temperature

Different crops require specific temperature ranges for optimal growth. Our AI analyzes historical temperature data to recommend crops suited to your local climate.

Rainfall

Water requirements vary significantly between crops. Our system considers your region's rainfall patterns to suggest crops that will thrive with available water resources.

Soil Composition

Soil type and nutrient content are crucial for crop success. Our AI analyzes your soil data to recommend crops that will flourish in your specific soil conditions.

Sunlight

Some crops require full sun, while others thrive in partial shade. Our system factors in your location's sunlight patterns when making recommendations.
