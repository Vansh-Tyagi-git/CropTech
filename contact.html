FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common Questions About CropTech

How does CropTech's AI technology work?

CropTech uses advanced machine learning algorithms that analyze data from various sources including satellite imagery, IoT sensors, weather stations, and historical crop performance. This data is processed to provide actionable insights and recommendations for farmers to optimize their operations.

Do I need special equipment to use CropTech?

While CropTech can work with basic information input manually, for the best results we recommend using IoT sensors and weather stations that can be purchased through our platform. These devices help collect real-time data from your fields for more accurate recommendations.

How much does CropTech cost?

CropTech offers several pricing tiers based on farm size and feature requirements. We have plans starting from $29/month for small farms up to custom enterprise solutions for large agricultural operations. Contact our sales team for a personalized quote.

Can CropTech integrate with my existing farm management software?

Yes, CropTech is designed to integrate with most popular farm management software through our API. We currently support direct integration with John Deere Operations Center, Climate FieldView, and several other platforms. Our technical team can help with custom integrations if needed.
